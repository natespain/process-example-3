The Client

Who is Lighthouse Ministries? What do they do?

Lighthouse Ministries is set apart by the Spirit of God to communicate the Gospel of Jesus Christ to meet the physical, emotional, and other needs of the poor and at-risk community in Lakeland, Florida. Their vision is for the individuals they serve to achieve an overall outcome of God's peace, life purpose, and fulfillment of potential.

Officially opened in 1977, Lighthouse Ministries has grown immensly. Currently they have operations that provide a quick place to sleep or a program that gives either a male or a female the ability to be educated and earn a job and career. They provide childcare and tutoring for families in the residential program and for families in the surrounding community.

The organization also owns several stores. These Family Stores are drop off places for donations. Much like Goodwill, these donations are then sorted and sold. The employees at the Family Stores are mostly residents in the educational program.
