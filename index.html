The Client

Who is Lighthouse Ministries? What do they do?

Lighthouse Ministries is a nonprofit located in Lakeland, Florida. Their aim is to serve the homeless and less fortunate community. The organization has been serving for over 40 years, and the current website shows the age of the company.

The Design Challenge

What is the design challenge?

The Design Challenge comes from a study done by IDEO. Learning the challenge helps the problem become actionable. The best way to learn the challenge is to get into the head of the user. This requires a look at the goals of the company and of the user.

  • Read More

The Research

What information can be learned?

The research is completed in two phases. The first phase is learning of the organization's existing strategies for advertising and communication. The second phase includes a look at current articles and innovations that directly relate to the challenges faced by this organization.

  • Read More

The Interviews

Who should be interviewed? Why do we interview?

One employee is interviewed to better understand the goals of the company and the current techniques being used. Two additional people are interviewed. Both of these two users have been to the website previously and sought specific information upon arrival on the website.

The Goals

What are the goals? What do we want to achieve on the website?

Sticky notes are used to explore the most important goals for the Lighthouse Ministries website. This first step requires narrowing down the user of the website. The Lighthouse Website attracts two main user categories so clarification has to be made.

  • Read More

The Solution

What is the solution? What will the final product look like?

By pulling all of the information together, both a Style Tile and a Call to Action can be created. The Style Tile lays out the general brand information that will be pulled throughout the new website. The Call to Action is the button that will pull the user in and achieve the goal of the website.
